Dr. C.Palanivelu

MS, MCh, DNB, FACS, FRCS(Hon) Ed.

Chairman GEM Hospital and Research Centre

Dr Palanivelu was first to introduce Iaparoscopic surgery in south India. He was instrumental in popularizing this new branch of medicine in India. He applied Iaparoscopic technique to all types of surgery including cancer surgery

Awards:

Dr BC Roy National Awardee under the category of development of specialty medicine namely laparoscopic surgery in 2006.

First Indian to win Silver Medal in the international Olympic minimal access surgery competition in 2009 in Phoenix USA.
